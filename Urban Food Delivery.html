How it works?

Urban Food Delivery promises to deliver good eats from South St. Pete restaurants featuring everything from $2.00 hollas, thick plates of stick to your ribs soul food, fresh and perfectly seasoned blue crabs, or fresh made to order salads whether you are at home, work, or at a sporting event.

Ordering Instructions

You can order from our menu on the website here at www.urbanfooddelivery.com by adding your food and customizing your order in the checkout cart OR by Downloading the "Urban Food Delivery" App and paying the $5.00 delivery fee.

Home of the $1.00 drinks all the time. 

Check out our list of restaurants if you don't see your favorite let us know.

If you want to order from more than (1) restaurant read our Help page under "Pricing".

Want to pay cash?  After your 1st order you can setup an account here on our website and once your identity is verified you can call in your order and pay for your order and $5.00 delivery fee in person by debit, credit, or cash in person.

Tip not included.  Driver does not carry cash.
